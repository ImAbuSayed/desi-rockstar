[Hook 1]

[Hook 2]
Stepping through the shadows in your golden dress
Every single motion leaves me breathless
Midnight city lights shining in your eyes

[Chorus]

Saari di saari umar de daan main tainu

[Verse 2]
Designer silhouettes painting the dark

Leaving everyone wanting a little bit more

Sleek automobiles glowing under the light
Heartbreak and passion a toxic embrace
I am lost completely in the beauty of your face
